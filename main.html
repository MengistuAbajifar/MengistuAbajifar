    by MENGISTU ABAJIFAR

    This are collection of creative, introspective, humorous poetry by Mengistu Abajifar. Spanning decades, the poems in this collection offer readers thoughtful insights on life, culture and humanity. From musings about everyday occurrences to cultivate understanding of different backgrounds and worldviews these poems provide comfort and amusement to all. So, come and delve into the delightful world of Mengistu Abajifar's poetry
